History of Computers (Ordinary Level)

📌 What is Computer History?

Computer history is the study of how computers have changed and improved over time.

🧠 Generations of Computers

1st Generation

  • Used vacuum tubes
  • Very large and slow

2nd Generation

  • Used transistors
  • Smaller and faster

3rd Generation

  • Used integrated circuits
  • More reliable

4th Generation

  • Uses microprocessors
  • Modern computers and laptops

⚡ Improvements

  • Computers got smaller
  • Computers got faster
  • More storage space

💡 Example Question

Name one feature of 1st generation computers.

They used vacuum tubes and were very large.

🧪 Exam Questions

Q1: What is a generation of computers? (3 marks)

Answer: A stage in the development of computers.

Q2: Name one feature of second generation computers. (3 marks)

Answer: They used transistors.

Q3: Give one improvement in modern computers. (3 marks)

Answer: They are faster or smaller.

🎯 Exam Tips

  • Learn generations in order (1st → 4th)
  • Memorise key words: vacuum tubes, transistors, ICs, microprocessors
  • Keep answers short and simple
  • No need for long explanations in OL
