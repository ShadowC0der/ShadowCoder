🔥 H1 Notes (Higher Level)

Comprehension (H1)
Analyse tone, inference, language techniques. Always quote and explain effect.
Composition (H1)
Structured argument, strong vocabulary, clear introduction and conclusion.
Poetry (H1)
Deep analysis of imagery, tone shifts, and poet’s intention.

📘 O1 Notes (Ordinary Level)

Comprehension (O1)
Clear, direct answers based on the text. No over-complication.
Composition (O1)
Simple structure: intro, body, conclusion.
Poetry (O1)
Basic themes, simple imagery explanation.
