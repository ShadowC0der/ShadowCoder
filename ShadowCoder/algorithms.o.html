Algorithms (Ordinary Level)

📌 What is an Algorithm?

An algorithm is a step-by-step set of instructions used to solve a problem or complete a task.

⚙️ Key Features

  • It has steps
  • It starts and ends
  • It solves a problem
  • It is clear and ordered

💡 Example

Making a cup of tea:

  1. Boil water
  2. Put tea bag in cup
  3. Pour water
  4. Add milk/sugar
  5. Drink tea

🔁 Simple Types of Algorithms

Linear Search

Check each item one by one until you find what you want.

Binary Search

Works by splitting sorted data into halves to find items faster.

🧪 Exam Questions

Q1: What is an algorithm? (3 marks)

Answer: A step-by-step set of instructions used to solve a problem.

Q2: Give one example of an algorithm. (3 marks)

Answer: Making a cup of tea or following a recipe.

🎯 Exam Tips

  • Keep answers short and simple
  • Use everyday examples (tea, recipes, instructions)
  • No need for technical terms like “efficiency”
